Let N = 12 = 2^2 + 2^3
Given that M^2 = 51 (mod 59), what is M^12 (mod 59)?

binary expansion of 12: (1100)

M^12 (mod 59)   = (M^2^3 mod 59) * (M^2^2 mod 59) (mod 59)
                = (51^3 mod 59) * (51^2 mod 59) (mod 59)
                = (19 * 5) (mod 59)
                = (95) (mod 59)
                = 36
